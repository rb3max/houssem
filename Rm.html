Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, commonly known as Cristiano Ronaldo or Ronaldo and nicknamed CR7, born February 5, 1985 in Funchal, is a Portuguese international footballer who plays as a striker at Al-Nassr FC. Considered one of the best footballers in history, he is with Lionel Messi (with whom he maintains a sporting rivalry) one of only two to have won the Ballon d'Or at least five times. Scorer of more than 800 goals in more than 1,100 career games, Ronaldo is the top scorer in football history according to FIFA. He is also the top scorer in the UEFA Champions League, European Cups, Real Madrid, Madrid derby, FIFA Club World Cup and Portugal, of which he is the official captain since 2008. The first player to have won the European Golden Shoe four times, he is also the top scorer in the history of the European Nations Championship ahead of Michel Platini and holds the record for goals in the national team, with 122 achievements.
